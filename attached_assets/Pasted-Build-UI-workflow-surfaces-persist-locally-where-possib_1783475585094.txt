The Big Vision in One Passage

Pro Se Navigator is being built as an AI-powered litigation operating system for self-represented litigants — a premium command center where a user can start a case through guided onboarding, select their matter type and posture, build a structured case memory, map facts and evidence into a timeline, track service and default risk, monitor docket deadlines, organize discovery, respond to motions and court orders, verify case law, review drafts, assemble document packets, build administrative-process records, and evaluate settlement leverage. The app is designed to be safe and legally responsible: it provides legal information, educational drafting support, procedural organization, and verification checklists, but does not act as a lawyer, does not give legal advice, and does not guarantee outcomes. Its deeper mechanism is a structured case workspace connected to playbooks, procedural-risk systems, citation-verification workflows, evidence mapping, deadline intelligence, and eventually specialized AI agents. Before API/database integrations are ready, the app is being built with visible no-API modules and local persistence so the product experience can keep advancing. Later, the same workflows can connect to real database persistence, OpenAI/Anthropic/Perplexity, CourtListener, RECAP/PACER, Stripe, affiliate systems, document export, and source-grounded legal research. The ultimate goal is to give pro se users a professional-grade operating system for organizing, understanding, and advancing their legal matters — not by pretending to be a lawyer, but by making the litigation process more structured, less chaotic, safer, and more navigable.
